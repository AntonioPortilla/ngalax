IT Segurity Consulting

Ethic al hacking is a preventive approach to information security: you try to break into your own system to test how secure it is, and how reactive your security specialists are.

Ethic al hacking is similar to car industry crash tests - it is impossible to verify how well an airbag in your car will work without conducting a simulated accident in laboratory. Should an airbag fail to work properly during a real accident it will be already too late to take a corrective action.

NGalax is a leading Swiss company, providing information security services such as ethic al hacking by penetration testing. Entirely vendor and product independent, we are committed to delivering the highest quality of service to our clients.
